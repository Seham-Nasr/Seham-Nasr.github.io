About Me

Hey, I'm Seham! I work as a data science researcher, and a PhD candidate at the Center for Cognitive Interaction Technology (CITEC) - Bielefeld University, Germany.

My research interest is to build an Explainable AI systems to improve transparency, trustworthy, and understandability.

Welcome to my corner of the web, where I share my writing, projects, tutorials, art, and whatever else I feel like putting out there.
