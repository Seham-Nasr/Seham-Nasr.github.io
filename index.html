About Me

Hey, I'm Seham! I work as a data science researcher, and a PhD candidate at the Center for Cognitive Interaction Technology (CITEC) - Bielefeld University, Germany.



Welcome to my corner of the web, where I share my writing, projects, tutorials, art, and whatever else I feel like putting out there. Here, you'll find my notes and a collection of technical articles I've written over the years. Visit the projects page to explore highlights of my open-source contributions.

This site is free of ads, affiliate links, tracking, sponsored posts, and paywalls. It's a space for self-expression and sharing what I've learned with the world. I want to inspire others to create their own creative space online, standing strong against the declining quality of the internet.
